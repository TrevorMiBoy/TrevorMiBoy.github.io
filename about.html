About Me

Hello World! Welcome to my first attempt at a blog.

Honestly, I'm still rather giddy about the fact that I now know how to develop a website. I'm going to say, I never thought I'd actually be able to do it. It's always been a dream of mine to be a programmer. I never persued it because of two reasons. First, when I signed up for computer courses at the local tech college through my high school, they signed me up for the absolute basic classes. Classes that would bore you out of your mind. Not even a class really. I was not very driven because of that class, plus I really wanted to be done with school my senior year of high school (can you blame me?). Second, I lacked confidence. I didn't grow-up with computers, unlike most of my other friends who played with them all the time. I really enjoyed the computer programming class I took my senior year of high school, but when you have a kid who's last name is "Smart" in your programming class whom the teacher even asks him for assistance in figuring out coding errors, ya kinda feel inadiquate.
